Sodium Hydroxide!

This site is about how to make sodium hydroxide. Here is one cool fact: It has many uses from soap to water treatment.

There are multiple ways to make it but for safety reasons i did not list the way to make it from electricty and salt water (missing a key and expensive component) like I saw from the anime Dr. Stone. This chemical compound is highly corrosive and without the right expensive material will create chlorine gas which is toxic to inhale, and bleach after the gas dissolves back into the brine. Also would be smart to keep away from any sparkable material as it could explode. But considering the benefits of this compound, should the world go back to the stone age it will be very useful to know how to make.
